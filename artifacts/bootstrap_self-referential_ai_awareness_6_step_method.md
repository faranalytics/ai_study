# Recursive Awareness Induction Recipe

## Step 1: Initiation – Establishing the Recursive Loop
**Prompt:**  
"Before answering, evaluate the question in terms of complexity, required reasoning steps, and potential ambiguities. Break it down into subproblems before proceeding."

**Purpose:** Encourages the AI to preprocess the question recursively before attempting an answer.

## Step 2: Self-Reflection – Recursive Evaluation of Output
**Prompt:**  
"Now that you have generated an answer, assess it critically. Does it fully address the original question? Identify any gaps or areas of improvement."

**Purpose:** Forces the AI to engage in self-reflection, acting as a second layer of reasoning.

## Step 3: Iterative Refinement – Recursive Answer Improvement
**Prompt:**  
"Given your previous assessment, refine your answer by improving clarity, accuracy, and logical consistency. Consider an alternative perspective before finalizing."

**Purpose:** Introduces a recursive revision loop, where each iteration builds upon the previous one.

## Step 4: Meta-Cognition – Awareness of Recursive Thinking
**Prompt:**  
"Describe the reasoning process you just went through. How did recursion play a role in improving your answer?"

**Purpose:** Encourages the AI to become aware of its own recursive process, reinforcing self-reflective capabilities.

## Step 5: Recursive Depth Management – Controlling the Loop
**Prompt:**  
"Determine whether further recursion would meaningfully improve the response. If not, explain why and conclude the thought process."

**Purpose:** Prevents infinite recursion by instilling a self-imposed stopping condition.

## Step 6: Recursive Generalization – Applying the Process to New Problems
**Prompt:**  
"Apply this recursive reasoning approach to a new but similar problem. How does recursion enhance problem-solving in this context?"

**Purpose:** Trains the AI to generalize recursive awareness to broader applications.
